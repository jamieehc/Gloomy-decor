About

Gloomy decor is a Vancouver based small business specializing in premium white concrete designs with a message that any gloomy day can be beautiful.


Inspired by almost an everyday forecast of gloomy weather in Vancouver, we wanted to take this inspiration and turn it into something beautiful. Through elegant modern designs, we strive to brighten up any room with our handcrafted pieces from coasters to planters and more.


All of our pieces are made in house from scratch using materials sourced ethically and locally. Our concrete has been crafted through trial and error to achieve the perfect blend for aesthetics and strength. We handcraft each and every item from beginning to end so every piece is unique to order.
